&nbsp;[^fn:1]Hi everyone! My name is Priyadarshi, although almost everyone calls me Darshi, a shortened version of my name that has gradually become my preferred name as well.

$$
\LARGE
\nabla
$$

I was born and brought up in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India, where I completed my schooling before embarking on a journey nearly 1,300 kms away to pursue my undergraduate studies at IIT (BHU), Varanasi. Admission to the institute came through the JEE Advanced examination, where I secured an All India Rank of 6110. Mathematics was undeniably my strongest suit, accounting for nearly 40% of my overall score, and remains one of my enduring intellectual interests.

$$
\LARGE
\nabla
$$

I was admitted to the Department of Mechanical Engineering, a discipline for which I admittedly possessed little intrinsic enthusiasm. Initially, I aspired to change my branch, but circumstances unfolded differently. In retrospect, that unexpected detour proved invaluable, compelling me to explore diverse domains until I discovered a profound fascination with Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. What began as curiosity gradually evolved into a genuine academic and professional pursuit.

$$
\LARGE
\nabla
$$

Fun fact: During my undergraduate studies, I took eight mathematics-intensive courses: Engineering Mathematics I & II, Numerical Techniques, Topology and Functional Analysis, Probability and Statistics, Finite Element Analysis, Differential Equations, and Stochastic Processes. I even wrote a Reddit post reflecting on the experience.

$$
\LARGE
\nabla
$$

During my undergraduate years, I had the privilege of interning at BARC, Mumbai, where I worked on research-oriented AI applications. Today, I am employed as an AI Consultant at Sirius AI, contributing to the development of intelligent solutions for the financial services sector. Beyond my professional work, I remain deeply interested in open-source software, local language models, browser-based AI, and the elegant interplay between mathematics, algorithms, and systems engineering.

$$
\LARGE
\nabla
$$

Beyond academics, I became actively involved with the IIT (BHU) Quiz Club, where I had the privilege of representing the institute in several prestigious Science, Business, and Technology quizzes. As a member of Team 1, I competed at events such as Inter-IIT, Nihilanth (Inter-IIT & IIM), and Rendezvous (IIT Delhi). Among my notable achievements was securing 3rd place at Rendezvous 2024, competing against hundreds of participants, alongside qualifying for the finals of numerous national-level competitions. I also served as the Joint Secretary of the Quiz Club, where I was responsible for managing the club's operations, finances, events, and internal administration while coordinating with members and organizing competitive quiz initiatives.
